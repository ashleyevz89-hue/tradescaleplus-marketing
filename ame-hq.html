Control centre FAQ

Control centre, answered.

Can I turn AME off completely?
Yes — one master switch in AME HQ. She stops replying, stops chasing, stops calling. Resume when you want. Customers still see the inbox + the dashboard, AME just pauses.
What if my staff want to use AME differently?
You can give staff different roles — full access, inbox-only, view-only. We log who did what, so accountability is clear.
Can I see what she nearly did?
Yes. Draft mode shows every reply she WOULD have sent on autopilot. You can audit her judgement before flipping any capability live.
Do I get a daily summary?
Yes. Optional daily summary email each morning — yesterday's calls, replies, bookings, anything that needs your attention.
What about data export?
Full CSV export of every customer, conversation, vehicle, sale, aftersales touch — any time, no extra cost.
