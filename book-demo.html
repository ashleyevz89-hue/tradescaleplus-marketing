Common questions

About the demo.

Most of what dealers ask before booking. If yours isn't here, ping us on the contact page.

Or just call us → 0800 ····
Do I need to prepare anything?
No. Tell us your dealership website when you book — we load your stock and have AME ready before you join.
Who should be on the call?
Whoever decides on tools. Most demos are 1-on-1 with the owner. Some bring a sales manager or partner. We're easy.
Will you try to sell me on the call?
No. We show you what AME does and answer questions. If it's a fit, we book a follow-up to set up onboarding. If not, we shake hands and move on.
What if my website doesn't have a stock feed?
We capture it directly using our Chrome extension. Works on any UK dealer site, including Cloudflare-protected ones. Zero setup on your end.
Can I see AME take a real call?
Yes — we'll call AME live during the demo so you can hear her handle an enquiry on your stock.
How quickly can I be live?
Most dealers go live in 5–7 days after the demo. Stock + Twilio + Vapi connection takes about 30 mins of your time spread across the week.
