Why this exists

The gap I kept seeing.

I spent years watching independent used-car dealers across the UK juggle four tools to do what one good admin would do — and miss calls anyway because nobody's at the desk on a Sunday afternoon.

The big chains have whole AI teams. Dealers in Llanelli, Bridgend, Carmarthen, Pembroke — they don't. They have a forecourt, a phone, a Facebook page, and one or two people doing everything. The CRM they pay for doesn't pick up the phone. The phone service they pay for doesn't update their pipeline. The review tool doesn't know what cars they sold.

So I built one thing that does all of it. One login. One bill. One AI doing the work of a part-time admin, a call-answering service, a CRM, a review tool, a social scheduler, and an aftersales system — for less than the cost of any one of those alone.

She's called AME — Automated Marketing Engine. She picks up your phone, replies to every channel, books test drives, chases cold leads, runs aftersales, drafts your reviews, and tells you how your month is going.

I built her with indie dealers, not for them. The first half-dozen dealers who let me into their world shaped what she does and what she doesn't. They told me what they actually needed at 7pm on a Tuesday — and that's what we built.

If you run an indie used-car business and you're tired of stitching tools together, book a 15-minute demo. I'll show you what AME does on your real stock, you'll decide in five minutes whether she's a fit, and we'll go from there.

— Ash

What we believe

Three things we hold to.

Not values posters. The decisions we make every week come back to these.

01.

Indie dealers come first.

Every feature we build, we ask: does this help a one-pitch dealer in Llanelli, or only a 12-site chain in Birmingham? If it doesn't help the first, we don't build it.

02.

One price. One bill. No bolt-ons.

Per-seat fees, setup costs, "premium" tiers — that's how legacy software grew its margins. We refuse. £499 buys everything we ship, forever.

03.

AI in the background, not the foreground.

AME does the work. You take the credit. Every message, every call, every review reply goes out in your dealership's name — never ours. The customer never knows you used AI.
