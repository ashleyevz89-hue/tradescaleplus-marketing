Six channels

Wherever they message, she's there.

Customers don't all use the same channel. Younger buyers want WhatsApp. Older ones still text. Some message your Facebook page. AME watches all of them.

SMS

Inbound + outbound on your dealership number via Twilio. Threading, recap, scheduling — all live.

WhatsApp

Your WhatsApp Business number, integrated direct. AME replies as you. Photos, voice notes, the lot.

Facebook Messenger

Every DM to your dealership Facebook page lands in your AME inbox. No app switching.

Instagram DM

Captures and threads every customer DM from your IG business account.

Web chat

AME-powered chat widget on your dealership website. Drops new enquiries into the inbox in real-time.

Email

Connect your Gmail. AME drafts replies, you approve. Threading + recipient management handled automatically.

Draft mode or autopilot

You decide how much she does.

Some dealers love reading every reply before it goes out. Others want their evenings back. AME runs in two modes — flip per channel, or per customer.

  • Draft mode (recommended for week 1)AME drafts every reply. You approve in two taps. Edit if you want, send if you're happy. Builds trust fast.
  • Autopilot modeAME sends directly. She still pings you if a customer asks something she's not sure about, or if a deal is stuck.

Inbox FAQ

Common inbox questions.

Can AME really sound like me?
Yes. In the first week we train her on your existing replies — your tone, your phrasings, your typical close. She'll feel like you wrote it. You correct any drafts that miss, she learns from each correction.
What if I want to take over a conversation?
Tap "Send manually" on any draft, or just open the conversation and type your own reply. AME steps back the moment you take over. She watches and resumes when you're done if you want.
Does she work in WhatsApp Business or regular WhatsApp?
WhatsApp Business. We help you set up the account in the first week.
What about Instagram comments — does she handle those?
Direct messages yes, public comments no (yet — on the roadmap).
Can I have different reply styles for different channels?
Yes. You can configure tone separately for SMS vs WhatsApp vs email vs web chat. Most dealers leave it identical.
What if she gets it wrong?
Every reply is logged with the AI activity history. If she sends something off-base, you can roll back, edit the draft post-hoc, or correct her training. It's all transparent.
