How she thinks

She doesn't guess. She knows.

AME isn't a chatbot reading off a script. She's grounded in your real data — your stock, your customers, your finance offers, your dealership voice — and she reasons against that every time someone reaches out.

If she doesn't know something, she doesn't make it up. She takes a message, escalates to you, and waits.

  • 1
    Reads the inboundSMS, WhatsApp, call transcript, email, web chat — classifies the intent (price enquiry, test-drive request, complaint, finance question, aftersales).
  • 2

  • 3
    Drafts or actsStraightforward → she replies in your tone and books the action. Needs you → she drafts and flags it for approval.
