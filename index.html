Meet AME

She runs your dealership while you sell cars.

AME is your AI sales admin — voice, text, and back-office in one. She never sleeps, never forgets a customer, and never lets a lead go cold.

Answers every call.

AI receptionist on your number 24/7. Captures leads, books test drives, sends recap via SMS. You hear every transcript.

Replies on every channel.

SMS, WhatsApp, Facebook Messenger, Instagram DM, web chat, email — one inbox. AME drafts or auto-replies. You stay in control.

Books test drives.

She checks your calendar, suggests slots, confirms with the customer, and adds it to your diary. With reminders.

Chases cold leads.

Every enquiry that's gone 14 days quiet gets a polite nudge in your voice. Wakes deals you'd forgotten about.

Knows your stock.

Learns every car the moment you list it. Reg, mileage, spec, photos. Answers customer questions before they ask twice.

Looks after your customers.

Day 7 check-in, Day 30 review request, MOT reminders, anniversary nudges. Aftersales runs itself.

Replies to your reviews.

Drafts on-brand responses to every Google + Facebook review. You approve in one tap.

Drafts your social posts.

Spots new stock, writes the post, picks the photo, queues it for Facebook + Instagram. You approve or edit.

Forecasts your month.

End-of-month forecast based on pipeline + aftersales + stock. Tells you what to chase to hit target.
