How she thinks

She doesn't guess. She knows.

AME isn't a chatbot reading off a script. She's grounded in your real data — your stock, your customers, your finance offers, your dealership voice — and she reasons against that every time someone reaches out.

If she doesn't know something, she doesn't make it up. She takes a message, escalates to you, and waits for your call.

  • 1
    Reads the inboundSMS, WhatsApp, call transcript, email, web chat — she classifies the intent (price enquiry, test-drive request, complaint, finance question, aftersales).
  • 2
    Pulls contextYour stock, the customer's history with you, their finance preferences, the deal stage they're in — assembled into a single picture.
  • 3
    Drafts or actsIf it's straightforward, she replies in your tone and books the action. If it needs you, she drafts and flags it for approval.
  • 4
    Logs everythingEvery action timestamped on the customer card. Every reply audited. Every decision reversible.

You stay in control

She works for you, not the other way around.

Every capability is a switch in AME HQ. Roll one out at a time. Pause when you need to. Audit every action she's taken in the last 30 days. We default everything to draft mode so nothing goes live until you've seen it.

When she's good, flip her to autopilot per channel. When something feels off, flip her back. Her tone, her hours, the channels she's allowed to send on — all configurable, all reversible.

  • Per-capability draft / autopilot toggle
  • Full audit log — every action, timestamped + reversible
  • Tone & working hours per channel
  • Escalation rules (complaints, finance, anything new)
