Stock FAQ

Stock questions, answered.

What if my website doesn't have a stock feed?
We use the Chrome extension or scrape your stock pages directly. Works on any UK dealer site, including ones behind Cloudflare. Zero setup on your end.
How fast does new stock appear in AME?
Within 15 minutes of you publishing it. For brand-new captures via the Chrome extension, it's instant.
Does she check MOT + tax via DVLA?
Yes. Every reg gets a DVLA Vehicle Enquiry API lookup — make, model, year, fuel, MOT status, tax status. Saves you re-typing.
What about photos?
Pulled from your website automatically. For Chrome-extension captures, photos are pulled from the listing source.
Can I edit a vehicle in AME?
Yes. Any vehicle in AME can be manually edited — price, description, photos, status. Changes don't get overwritten by the next sync if you've manually edited.
