What she does on calls

Six things she handles. Without you.

The stuff that fills your day. AME handles every one of them on a phone call, then logs it back into the dashboard for you.

Captures new leads.

Name, number, vehicle of interest, finance need. Logged in the dashboard as a customer card before you've checked your phone.

Books test drives.

She looks at your diary, suggests slots, confirms with the customer, sends a confirmation SMS with the dealership address. Done in 30 seconds.

MOT booking, service slots, parts enquiries — AME captures the request and routes it to you with a one-tap reply.

Voice FAQ

The voice questions.

Will it sound like a robot?
No. AME uses high-fidelity voice synthesis tuned to a natural UK accent. Most callers do not realise they are speaking to an AI — and we always let her introduce herself by name so it is honest.
What if a customer asks something AME can't answer?
She politely takes a message, logs the question against the customer card in your dashboard, and texts you a heads-up. You call back when you can.
Can she handle complaints?
She handles them gracefully and routes immediately to you. No complaint gets brushed off — it lands in your inbox flagged red.
Does she record calls?
Yes, with consent. AME announces the recording at the start of every call. Recordings are stored in your dashboard for 90 days. Customer can request deletion any time (UK GDPR compliant).
Can I customise what she says?
Yes. The greeting, the dealership name, the booking flow, the finance script — all editable in the dashboard. We tune her with you in the first week.
What happens if Vapi goes down?
Calls fall back to your normal voicemail. You will not lose the call entirely. We will also email you any failures within 5 minutes.
